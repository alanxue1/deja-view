Privacy Policy for Deja View

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Deja View ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

Data Collection

Deja View collects Pinterest board data for the purpose of generating 3D models and room visualizations. Specifically:

  • We access Pinterest pins and board information that you authorize through Pinterest OAuth
  • We process image data from Pinterest pins to generate 3D model descriptions
  • We do not store personal information beyond what is necessary for processing

Data Usage

Data collected is used solely for:

  • Processing Pinterest pins and generating 3D model descriptions
  • Creating room visualizations based on furniture and design elements
  • Improving our service functionality

Third-Party Services

Deja View integrates with:

  • Pinterest API: To access board and pin data (requires your authorization)
  • OpenAI API: To generate descriptions and analyze furniture metadata

We do not share your data with third parties except as necessary to provide our service.

Data Storage

We do not permanently store your Pinterest data. Processing is done on-demand and data is not retained beyond the immediate processing session.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Pinterest API access at any time through your Pinterest account settings
  • Request information about what data we process
  • Discontinue use of our service at any time

Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data during processing. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
